Robin

The archaeologist who seeks to uncover the truth of the past! Born and raised in Ohara, the land of archaeology, Robin is a gifted scholar who earned her degree at a young age. Her dream is to read the Rio Poneglyph and learn the world's true history. Robin is the sole survivor of Ohara, destroyed by the government.
